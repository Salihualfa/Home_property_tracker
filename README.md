# 📦 Property Tracker – Shop & Room Inventory Manager

A beautiful, offline-ready Progressive Web App (PWA) for tracking your shop, room, or personal inventory. Built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript – no frameworks, no dependencies, no server required.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📦 Inventory Management
- ✅ Add, edit, and delete property items
- ✅ Mark items as **Present** or **Missing**
- ✅ **Auto-alerts** when items are missing (visual + audio)
- ✅ Search, filter by status, and filter by category
- ✅ Export/Import data as JSON (backup & restore)

### 🛒 Shopping List
- ✅ Add items you plan to buy in the future
- ✅ Set estimated prices for each item
- ✅ **Automatic total cost calculation** – updates when you add, mark as purchased, or delete
- ✅ Mark items as **Purchased** (removes from total)
- ✅ Clear all purchased items with one click
- ✅ Add items directly from your inventory

### 📱 PWA Support (Progressive Web App)
- ✅ **Installable** on Android, iPhone, Windows, and Mac
- ✅ **Works offline** – no internet connection needed
- ✅ **Full-screen mode** – feels like a native app
- ✅ **Custom app icon** on your home screen
- ✅ **Fast loading** – cached for instant access

### 🎨 Design
- ✅ Beautiful dark gradient background with animated particles
- ✅ Glassmorphism UI (frosted glass effect)
- ✅ Gold accent colors for important elements
- ✅ Smooth animations and transitions
- ✅ Fully responsive – works on all screen sizes
